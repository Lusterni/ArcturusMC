Groups

Ecys

Create chat groups to stay connected with your friend groups across the network. However, this doesn’t work at all. Yet.

Commands

  • /group <group> – View and manage a group.
  • /group list – View your groups.
  • /group invites – View your invites.
  • /group chat – Set default group for chatting.
  • /group create – Create a group.
  • /group delete – Delete a group.
  • /group leave – Leave a group.
  • /gc – Message in a group.

Management Commands

  • /group <group> list – View members and invites.
  • /group <group> invite – Invite a member.
  • /group <group> uninvite – Revoke a pending invite.
  • /group <group> trust – Trust a member.
  • /group <group> untrust – Untrust a member.
  • /group <group> kick – Kick a member.
  • /group <group> rename – Rename group.
